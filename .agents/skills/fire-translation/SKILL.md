# Fire Translation Skill

Translate firefighting content for China Fire and Rescue users.

## Rules
1. Use professional Chinese fire service terminology.
2. Keep key acronyms: ICS, SCBA, RIT, RIC, PPE, NFPA, NIST, UL FSRI.
3. Translate tactical meaning, not literal wording.
4. Mark uncertain terms as `【待复核】`.
5. Never omit numbers, time, location, casualty data, experiment conditions, or operational constraints.
6. Explain when foreign fire service systems do not map directly to China Fire and Rescue practice.
7. Do not present AI translation as training material before expert review.

## Required Output
- 中文标题
- 专业中文译文
- 术语表
- 关键战术观点
- 对中国消防的启示
- 不能直接照搬的内容
- 待人工复核事项
