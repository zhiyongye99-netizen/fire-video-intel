# Fire Intelligence Report Skill

Generate concise Chinese firefighting intelligence reports for commanders, training staff, and business departments.

## Report Principles
1. Prioritize operational value.
2. Separate facts, interpretation, and recommendations.
3. Highlight command, safety, training, equipment, and policy implications.
4. Do not overstate conclusions.
5. Flag foreign practices that conflict with Chinese conditions.
6. Keep all high-risk claims in `待人工复核` until reviewed.

## Standard Sections
1. 今日新增
2. 重点推荐
3. 技战术要点
4. 对中国消防启示
5. 术语更新
6. 待人工复核内容
7. 不宜直接照搬内容
